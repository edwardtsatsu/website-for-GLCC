Lead Pastors

Apostle Francis Andorful

Francis Andorful is a vibrant God-man who has been committed with an assignment from God to the nations. He is called into the Apostolic ministry with diverse undeniable proofs of God’s presence and validation in his ministry. He is a sought after conference speaker whose ministry is accompanied by signs, wonders, healings and the prophetic administrations of the Spirit of God. He has been a blessing to several ministries, churches and corporate entities through the well-crafted message of the knowledge of the Glory of the Lord and his teachings on the vital elements of our divine participation in the Godhead. His message delivers vital kingdom principles and keys to harnessing the believer’s administration of the glory of God in the earth.





Apostle Kingsley Nti

Kingsley Nti is a dynamic minister of the Gospel of Jesus Christ and a trained pharmacist with marketing expertise. Apostle Deep as he is affectionately called functions with a strong apostolic grace and has insight into the depths of God's Word of grace and accurate building of the new creation, communicating with Grace the seasoned Word of the Lord. He does so with surpassing clarity and practicality. As a man of wisdom, understanding and humility, he is affable and very friendly hence loved by those who encounter him. Apostle Kingsley has been in church leadership since his high school days with Scripture Union. In the University, he was the President of Unity Hall Christian Fellowship and then the Prayer Secretary of Inter Hall Christian Fellowship.





Apostle Prince Impraim

Apostle Prince is an embodiment of grace and wisdom in the dealings of God’s constructive wisdom. He is a dynamic preacher of the Word with strong emphasis on leadership, discipline and the supernatural power of God. His ministrations are accompanied with verifiable healings and miracles. He is the Lead Pastor of the Sunyani Glorylife City Church and oversees the World Harvest Missions Alliance of GAFoM. With this function under his arm, he is graced to engineer the productive outreach efforts of Glorylife and the multiplication of the gospel in the northern sector of Ghana. He has a strong passion for youth and youth ministers and as such has pioneered several campaigns and seminars in the tertiary institutions in and around Sunyani. Apostle Prince also ministers on Faith Channel, an online radio based in the UK. He is a Geologist by profession and works with the Newmont GoldCorp Ghana.
