THE MYSTICAL UNION

The incarnation of God in Christ is the seed of the mystical union that is ordained by God before the foundation of the earth. The incarnation is the test of the antichrist operation. 1 John 4:1-2 This mystical union is predicated on the ascension of Christ and the revelation of it is prophesied to be released in the outpouring of the Holy Spirit.....
