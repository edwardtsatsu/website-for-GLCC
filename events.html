Events

GLORY CELEBRATION SERVICES

As an apostolic household mandated to perfect the saints in full operational status as Sons of God, we have at the very center of our activities the Glory Celebration Services which hold on Sundays. These services are packed with wonderful times of prayer, heart moving worship and beautiful fellowship in the Word, testimonies and artistry. Come and join us in fellowship; we are one family that is ever growing and every fruitful. Our celebration services have the special feature of personal fellowship and consultations with any of our pastors over whatever you require assistance with. Check with available service times and join in for an encounter with God’s glory. Meeting Days: Sundays - 8am to 10.30am | 5pm – 8pm




FITNESS IN THE WORD

This is a special School of the Word Services held weekly for young ministers and growing Christians. This service comes with a special and unique delivery of the Word of God in such a manner your understanding and rebuilds your framework of interpretation and communication of divine realities. It is a highly recommended meeting for everyone seeking to know and understand the mystic operations of God in the dispensing of His glory on the earth and the believer’s participation in this process. Come and meet a solid faculty of wonderful teachers of the Word; who have built a strong integrity in their handling of the scriptures and have received the honour of testifying to the validity of the truth they share. Meeting Days: Accra Main Campus - Thursdays – 6pm to 9pm Contact Dean for more information: dean-fitw@glorylife.church




NIGHT OF GLORY

Obadiah 1:17 “But upon mount Zion shall be deliverance, and there shall be holiness; and the house of Jacob shall possess their possessions.” With the increasing spate of life’s many problems, the faith of the people of God must be safeguarded from despair. The revelation of a mighty God during the hard and trying times of life is a fountain of fresh water for the broken soul. Night of Glory is a monthly prophetic convocation that pulls together believers and unbelievers from all walks of life to seek and encounter the Power of God through healings, deliverance, prophetic manifestations and Word ministrations. Night of Glory has been blessed with several testimonies of answered prayer, prophetic fulfilments, miracle breakthroughs of all kinds and healing of diseases. You are set for your season of breakthrough!!! Meeting Time: Accra - First Friday of Every Month – 10pm to 4am Sunyani – Last Friday of Every Month – 10pm to 4am
