The Glory Mandate

The ministry mandate committed to us by the will of God is to build a systematic framework for the operation of God’s glory on the earth. By this, we are given grace from God to communicate accurate kingdom principles and philosophies that undergird the revelation of Christ in the last day’s church. From the mountains of Zion comes a company of glory carriers who have camped with the Lord on the high places of deep intimacy and divine encounters; and we carry the torch of the Lord’s heart for the nations in these end times. The journey on which we are set is what we gladly announce as the Glory Mandate.




Vision Statement

We are a community committed to raising an apostolic company of ministers / ministries by active fellowship in the graces of God and the teaching of gospel of the Kingdom of God and the Christ, in the end that they may be empowered to demonstrate the fullness of the Kingdom in absolute power, invading all spheres of society with the glory of God.




Mission Statement

To raise an apostolic company of ministers/ministries to demonstrate the Kingdom of God through the accurate dispensation of the knowledge of the glory of the Lord.
